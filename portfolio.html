BARKIPEDIA DOG INFORMATIONAL APPLICATION

Barkipedia is an app designed for dog lovers, with or without dogs, who want to gather dog information easily. Users can look at all of the events in the city that they can take their dog to, find reviews on various services like vets and groomers for their dogs, and look through forums containing specific questions that users have about dogs.

HOSPITAL MANAGEMENT

For this course, our group was given the user story of creating a system for a hospital where doctor's schedules and patient's appointments were disorganized using outdated methods. To solve this, my group created a system that managed Doctor's schedules and patient's appointments in one space. The system has 3 different user types (and thus 3 different interfaces): Doctor, Administrator, and Patient.

The importance of this group project was applying agile and scrum development to work as a team in an efficient manner.
